Alaskan Malamute

Originally, Alaskan Malamute is bred for hauling heavy freight because of their power an endurance.

  • Nickname: Mal or Mally
  • Litter size: 4-10 puppies
  • Life span: Up to 16 years
  • Coat: An outer guard coat and a plush undercoat

Alaskan Malamutes are heavy dogs. An adult male alaskan Malamute with approoriate build and traning can pull 500-1000 kilograms of weight. They are independent, resourceful and clever.
